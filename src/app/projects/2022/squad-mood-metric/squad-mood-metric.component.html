Squad Mood Metric

Splunk, Postman, Python

Jan 2022 - Apr 2022

Project Overview

The project involved bi-weekly collection and analysis of mood metrics from 10 teams to assess their productivity. Utilizing Python scripts, Postman, and Splunk, data was processed and visualized to guide leadership in resource allocation.

Business Impact

The project's impact was marked by a 10% increase in overall productivity, reflecting improvements in team efficiency and morale. This enhancement stemmed from data-driven resource allocation and proactive issue resolution, fostering a more effective and positive work environment.

Example on Displaying the Squad Mood Data.

The Challenges

To accomplish this project, my primary objective was to devise a method for data collection and visualization on a Splunk dashboard. The procedure started with the biweekly compilation of information into an Excel document. After that, I used Postman once a week to submit this data to Splunk.


The main challenge I had was modifying the data to satisfy the unique encapsulation requirements of the Splunk API before transmission. To start, I used Python to extract the data from Excel and save it as a CSV file. Then, I included further arguments that were necessary for the POST request.
